Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

No Personal Data Collected

Vivid Menu: Beyond Translation does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not track you in any way.

Camera Usage

The app uses your device camera solely to scan menus in real time. Images are processed on-device or sent to our translation service for analysis and are never stored or linked to your identity.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party APIs (e.g., translation or image recognition services) to power app features. These services receive only the image data needed to fulfil your request and are contractually prohibited from retaining or misusing it.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because we collect no personal data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.
