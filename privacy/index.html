Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Vivid Menu: Beyond Translation ("the App") is operated by Gengcong Yang. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We collect only what is necessary to operate the App.

Data We Collect

  • Anonymous device identifier. On first launch, the App signs in anonymously via Firebase Authentication. This generates a random user ID (not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID) that persists on-device. It is used solely to track your Discovery Point balance and subscription status.
  • Usage quota data. We store your Discovery Point balance, subscription status, and approximate token usage in Firebase Firestore, keyed to your anonymous ID.
  • Feedback you submit. If you choose to send feedback through the App, your message, anonymous user ID, app version, and submission timestamp are stored in Firestore. Feedback is used only to improve the App.
  • Menu images. When you scan a menu, the image is sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis. Images are not stored by us. Google's data-use terms apply to data processed through their API.
  • Purchase information. In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We receive only a StoreKit transaction receipt to verify your purchase; we do not receive your payment details.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or Apple ID
  • Your location
  • Any advertising or tracking identifiers
  • Any health or financial data

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and Firestore database. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Gemini API — Menu image analysis and translation. Gemini API Terms
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchase processing. Apple Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Firestore quota and feedback records are retained indefinitely to maintain service continuity. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Because we use anonymous identifiers only, no data is linked to a child's identity.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices change, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
